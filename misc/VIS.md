### Signs and symptoms
Initially, your skin sheds frequently accompanied with pain the size of an Asian Hornet. You will also have a large amount of brain fog, usually forgetting the hours of what they did during 6:00AM-7:00AM, 9:00AM-12:00PM, 2:00PM-6:00PM and 12:00AM-3:00AM. Brain fog is also accompanied with whispers of evil.
Additional symptoms include
•Hair loss
•insomnia
•vertigo
•black void warts growing in your back
•hallucinations of death and darkness
•insanity
•loss of your senses 
•body tissue falling off
p•arts of your limbs become dead



### Causes
VIS is caused by prolonged exposure to void matter, approximately 7 months of small exposure everyday and 7 minutes if in a 20ft radius or less. You can also gain VIS via physical touch of void matter and the body fully absorbing it. Or if you were to look at [[VIDO]]
