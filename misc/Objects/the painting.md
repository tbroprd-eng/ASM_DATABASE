### ABOUT'
The painting also known as "the picture of all", is a painting with some of the most powerful figures in reality in the clouds with white marble ruins, including [[scull]]. [[VIDO]], [[Xatrix]] and many other divine deities

There is no way to destroy the painting under any circumstance with timeline erasure, existence erasure and concept erasure somehow not affecting the painting whatsoever.
The painting is also unmovable, to any degree.
### FUNTION
if one of the Gods, entities, artifacts and weapons changes form or dies it changes on the painting, showing the live status of them
### LOCATION
The location of the painting always changes in 10 day cycle, usually being the middle of nowhere in areas such as, [[FVA]], True heaven and out of the bounds of reality. If you somehow encounter the painting, you will see it mounted in a wall, with its material being the same marble in the picture
