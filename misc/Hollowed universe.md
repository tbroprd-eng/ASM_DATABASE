Recent reports from live multiverse scrapings show a 98% spike of hollowed universes, which is a universe that has been affected with the hollow phenomenon where universes were erased internally by a specific event that was created by multiple life forms that were raised inside the universe for their entire lifetime without exiting their home universe.

### BEER
An interesting case of a hollowed phenomenon is a universe that was hollowed out with only the bar surviving. This case has been connected to [[VIDO]], due to recovered journals made by AD, on which he writes about on how a resurrected person kills 9 people on that bar claiming an apocalypse will come before being stoned to death. After 3 days, a figment of VIDO invades the universe, claiming and erasing all life that was contained on the universe, with only a singular bar in the middle of said bar.
