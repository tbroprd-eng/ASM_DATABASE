Hologramicos is a type of document  that was used During the periods of reality, mostly during the [[heavenly war]] by certain Deities and Entities. These documents were engraved into light, void and darkness, with the only way of unlocking an Hologramico is altering it in a specific order via light manipulation.

Hologramicos can be used as an advance encrypter for your files, just as long you do not change any sort of matter that could alter its structure indirectly.
