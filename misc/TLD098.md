TLD098(Timeline Destroyer 98) is a machine residing at Area ████, Sector ██, where it can erase objects, events, biological life and concepts with no jurisdiction(eg:, explosions, famine, dreaming etc. ) from all timelines.

Anyone nearby a person who uses TLD098 will still have memories of what they erased

### Instructions
For events and weak concepts, you would need to place a physical object connected to the event/concept, then you would need to pull lever A3 and B8, wait exactly 12.3 seconds and press button 3M. If steps were followed correctly, you would begin to hallucinate for 1 minute,

If you want to erase any object or biological life from every timeline, you would need to place the object inside the tube, after that, you must pull lever F3 and A2 and Button 2H it a span of 20 milliseconds. If done correctly, you would begin feeling chills around your body for 4 minutes.
