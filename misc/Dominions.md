## About
Dominions are techniques/abilities that are based on the persons personality and history and are hard to obtain to a regular person. They cant be obtained via power stealing/absorbing, and they cant be reversed engineered with magic of any kind.

### Domnions types and it's function
 The dominion gives the person special access to reality, essentially warping reality to there will. Usually dominions are assigned to 3 types A, B and C along with C-NA, with the most common dominion type is dominion A, weapons. This type of dominion is usually bounded to there life and soul so they have almost an indestructible weapon unless the holder of the dominion is close to death. The second type of dominion, dominion B, Powers. It essentially gives the holder of the dominion powers based on there previous power or personality. Also there is dominion C, Forms. It gives the holder of the dominion a physical or non-physical form that gives them alternate abilities of there current abilities they possessed. There is a hidden dominion type that is in Dominion C called Dominion C-NA, which is a sub dominion type that gives the holder of Dominion C-NA an awakened state which makes the user heavily buffed in every aspect, most of the time In multiversal level. When this dominion stops, the user losses consciousness due to the immense energy wasted most of the time killing them.

### How to obtain a dominion
Usually being a user of a dominion needs you to be a powerful being. Usually being able to destroy a country the size of America or a planet the size of Jupiter, but that's not all. You need to have to channel your energy in words, typically the word "dominion " in there native language to manifest it properly. You also need a proper motive to have a dominion in the first place and a stable mind or having a great spiral into madness and hatred that bends reality.



### ORIGINS
The origins of the creation of the dominion is unknown. But it is likely that it came from a giant energy source that was able to affect all existence.
