
The Citadel of misery commonly known as The Citadel, is a brutalist goth citadel located within the [[FVA]], being the heart for all the planning during the [[heavenly war]], including the assassination and death of ████. The Citadel has its own flow of time, Which


The Citadel of misery is 72,898 miles tall and 175,000 miles wide, with 100 floors, being the largest Citadel all across reality, including a top security prison that holds 53b prisoners till this day.

The Citadel was constructed by the [[VOT]], a coalition formed by multiple organizations with similar goals to reclaim what was theirs.

### Guards
The Citadel contains 193b guards, with 1.93 billion patrolling each floor, these guards have Void like abilities, similar to  ████'s abilities, but more "inferior", these abilities include but vary in multiple aspects:

•Liquid Mimicry
•Darkness Manipulation in a 100ft radius

Guards from the Citadel of misery are composed of Sapient life fused into sentient void matter created by ████ , with most guards having no recollection of their past life, but still contain old characteristics of their old personalities.


###  History
The Citadel was built by the VOT as a base of operations to the coalition, and a way to expand the FVA via linking it to the Citadel, turning it into a Space ship that would infect the nearby areas with pure void matter.
When the Heavenly war was declared by the VOT, they would weaponize the Citadel and temporarily double its height and width
in addition to its weaponization, the VOT would create Similar but smaller sizes of the Citadels and distribute them throughout reality, infecting everything nearby.
After the events of the Heavenly war, the VOT would dissolve, leaving the Citadel abandoned, with only the guard's patrolling the Citadel.
