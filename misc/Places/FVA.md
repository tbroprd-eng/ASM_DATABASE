 FVA also known as The forbidden void area is an area located below reality, this is also where the Pure manifestation of pure darkness and void  inhabits.

### Appearance.
The FVA is mostly pitch black, with floating islands made out of pure void matter, in these islands are places that were replaced with void in the main reality/timeline. Above the FVA you can see floating pieces of broken sky, with wallpaper like patterns.


### Abandoned ships
The FVA also have giant abandoned ships that were made by VIDO's army.
These ships are giant, with their usual dimensions being 40-200 universes tall and wide.

These ships were seen in multiple documents on where the ships exterminated 1000 universes per minute.
The ships are now frozen in time, with all inhabitants inside demanifested into their void liquid state.

### Landmarks
They're multiple Known Landmarks on the FVA, such as the [[Citadel of misery]], [[Dockyard of Limbo]], [[Tree of corruption]] and more.
