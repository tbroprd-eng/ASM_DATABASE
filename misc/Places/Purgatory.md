### About
Purgatory  is a place in reality where it is between  Heaven and Hell. It is a giant dimension made for lost ones that wonder reality who died unjudged and with unfinished business, bringing all wandering spirits across all of reality to this place.

Purgatory also known as PGTRY if entered will teleport you into a hall of doors, with each door sending you to a random dimension. PGTRY acts like a hub for all dimensions, that are available(eg:, Dimension of light, [[FVA]], [[Lovya]]'s Domain. ) and forcibly connects them to each other, regardless of logic, concept or danger, since PGTRY automatically patches any errors of reality without it damaging the user.
