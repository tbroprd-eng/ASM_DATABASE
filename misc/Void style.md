虛空風格 also known as void fighting style, is a technique that almost any species with life can do, and was early most seen by mortal beings in China due to how deadly the attacks were, and was later banned in use due to how deadly it was to who got hit by the attack. It was later re discovered by the soviets in the 1950s and was used for The 13th Department for decades until most of the people who used the void fighting style slowly developed [[VIS]] and later died because of it.



### History
when [[VIDO]] was in power during the [[heavenly war]], he altered reality so that in almost every single being, immortal or mortal, has the genetic code where if unlocked, they would become a puppet to VIDO
