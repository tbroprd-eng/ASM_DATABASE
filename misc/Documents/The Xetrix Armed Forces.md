#HEXAGON 
The Xetrix Armed Forces is a Military organization Founded and Managed by [[Xatrix]], and is comprised of [[xetrixes]] who follow a similar concept to military rankings. 
They would usually create camps at frozen universes, which were universes affected by reality warping and time manipulation. 

Camps that the XAF approved would contain 15 [[Xetrix-SC]]'s patrolling outside
