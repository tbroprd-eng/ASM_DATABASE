A Warped human is phenomenon where if an advance sapient is isolated in conditions where external stimuli is deprived or removed in the remainder of the isolation for 10-70 years their physical bodies begin to warp and bend with reality while having their own independent time that surrounds themselves.

### Rare Abilities
1 in 3 people who experienced Warped human develop abilities based of the previous area they were at before their isolation. People who gain these abilities are able to manipulate certain parts of reality for a temporary amount of time before needing to recharge(Takes 10-15 hours to recharge fully). As of right now there is at least 50 unique abilities documented, from most common in the bottom to the most rare.
