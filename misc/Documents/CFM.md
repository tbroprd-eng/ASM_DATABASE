### About
The Central flower Merger or CFM is a Government/cult like entity, with their goal to initiate the event called [[THE RESURRECTION]].

Along with their preparedness for the event of the resurrection, they would gather all 5 people who gained the essence of true life, and merge them into 1 giant ball of energy with one of them being [[Ferlucy]].

We have already neutralized CFM and seized all materials that were used to prepare for said event.
