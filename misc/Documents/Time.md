### Entia Aeternitatis
Entia Aeternitatis are entities who were previously mortal beings that are now forever devotees of time. They protect time for 1 reason, obsession. They see time as something not even their soul should even be worthy of worshiping of, often transcending into beings that are immortal in every single aspect being Entia Aeternitatis. They posses abilities similar to Machine Elves but not quite, they posses some type of form of time manipulation and immeasurable speed, often crashing into people who mess with time in any way shape or form. 


### The views
Time in reality is viewed  by most cultures as a Sacred and Holy concept, being the foundation of everything due to its importance of holding the structure of existence.
With most cultures, if something Sacred and Holy, they will protect it their blood and tears.
For some cultures and mythology, people and sometime Gods rebelled against the concept of time due to how they see time as a life parasite, eating there lifespan even though it has enough power to erase them. They would try to manipulate or even control time in some way that would benefit themselves, but it always ends in a catastrophic way either being killed or erased out of existence by Entia Aeternitatis or they would somehow end their universe with the most likely cause being their universe to turn into a large black hole.
