Codex lucis is a  6th century book from an alternate version of earth where they  created ftl travel and  magic. The book contains 60 pages along with 30 spells of light, with information about how to use them,  and was deciphered by the ASM.

### ORGINS
The orgin of the spells was cited in page 4, where it claims a divine being who is brighter than the sun decended down to talked about how light can save them from entities of darkness. Then the deity gave each person who was listening to him, a manual with spells and instructions, and a floating orb with infinite light and energy.  


## Directions on how to use the spells
You would need to hold a light source, the brighter it is the more effective it would be. Then you will need to think about brightness, and fill your mental eyes and brain with it. After that you can say  any of the 30 spells on the codex.
