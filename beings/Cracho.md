Cracho Pupulis is a officer of ASM, specifically being designated at Division-HTY, being a direct relative of [[6th God]]. He has no connected strings with the 6th God for unknown reasons. He wasn't controlled and manipulated by 6th God, with his only big connection with 6th God other than his origin being him is unlocking his demigod form.


### Demigod form
Cracho awakened his form during an incident during the [[GLITCHER]] massacre. With Cracho awakening, it caused collateral damage in a radius of 10,000 mile, emitting 6m Joules during the transformation.

Cracho's Demigod form named CG, gives Cracho 4 tendrils that are 4ft that shift across his eye, these tendrils are made with pure chaotic energy, eviscerating and evaporating everyone in contact with the tendrils except if they are in Cracho's mental whitelist which negates all damage to people Cracho trusts.


Cracho also is also able to summon sentient gasses from his tendrils, these gasses are able to burn any sort of life, poisoning them if inhaled.
