### About 
Name: Lovya Urania
True Age: 490Qd years old
Classification: Deity of Concept
Aliases: Aphrodite, Venus, Love
Dimension of Origin: Unknown
Primary Occupation: Goddess of Love
Affiliation: ASM, 
Core Directive: Maintain Love across reality
Current Status: Alive

Lovya Urania is a Goddess who you guessed it, is the Deity of love, being able to control love all across reality, even being able to influence deities who are weaker or on the same level as her. Lovya is often mistaken to be made from [[HALLOWGEN]], due to her massive ties to reality.

###  Location
Lovya resides at [[Purgatory]], specifically at the 244 door when you turn right at the start at purgatory.

Lovya's domain is 300ft tall and 500ft wide. Along with this, clouds of blue and pink accompany the room, these clouds if inhaled increase dopamine levels with no real consequences.
