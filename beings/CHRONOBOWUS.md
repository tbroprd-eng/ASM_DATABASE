### About 
Name: Chronobowus
True Age: 798Qd
Classification: Deity of Concept
Aliases: Chronos, time
Dimension of Origin: Manifested by [[HALLOWGEN]]
Primary Occupation: God of time
Affiliation: 4 main concepts
Core Directive: Make sure time is in a state of balance
Current Status: incarnated into an unknown individual


Chronobowus is the direct manifestation/incarnation of time itself, created by [[HALLOWGEN]]. Being able to manipulate time in any given form, and also having abilities which can create new rules for time in an unknown amount of range.

### Personality and role
Chronobowus personality is seen to be similar to a tired, semi serious lawyer, telling all info 


he is the only DOC that survived the massacre of the 4 main DOC's, turning himself into a grandfather clock, being the size of 90 multiverses located in an unknown area, with his main consciousness and memories being transported into an unknown individual.

### CHRONOB
Chronob is what is left of Chronobowus. With Chronob gaining sentience after 2 months of the incarnation of Chronobowus.

Chronobs personality is viewed to be a strict authoritative figure for maintaining the causality of time and other aspects of it. Since Chronob's role to maintain time, there has been a decrease of time travellers and timeline hopping, due to Chronob seeing it as a danger to reality and concepts inside it.
