The Glitcher  is an anomaly comprised out of things that are either mistakes or physical matter Glitcher corrupted.


### RANDOMIZATION  
The Glitcher contains the ability to randomize areas of reality in a 100ft radius with the following aspects that changes when he uses said ability.

• Deletion of a random spot of existence
• Add, Remove, rewrite and swap concepts 
• Rewrite the narrative
• Corrupt areas of reality 
• swap all solid, liquid and gas  ,
• alter biological life

### THE DIMENSION OF THE FORGOTTEN
There is a pocket dimension owned by The Glitcher called "The dimension of the forgotten" named by the first group of people who discovered his dimension, where the Glitcher can enter via his own body mass, essentially becoming a portal. Inside said pocket dimension are Concepts, People, Solid Matter and other things that were forgotten, erased, and mistakes of reality. We have studied that there is zero gravity and no concept of death not caused by The Glitcher in this pocket dimension due to unknown circumstances.

The Glitcher talks about that this dimension used to be his prison for his 400.8 quadrillion years where he was in great pain and suffering, and how he would plot revenge for his maker, which is a direct descendent of ████ and  ████ that has the strongest energy of their essence.


### MASS KILLINGS 
After The Glitcher was broken free from his dimension where he was kept a prisoner for almost his entire life, he started terminating groups of people all across reality, usually at times when Nocturnal Fauna are conscious or at an area that contains fog. 

Some survivors claim that before they were almost about to be killed, they would feel massive amounts of déjà vu, this is the cause of the Glitcher eliminating available timelines on where they would escape and survive, with the timeline deletions bleeding into peoples brains(it is also to note that the estimate per person is 26 trillion to 30 quadrillion timelines erased). 
Survivors also reported that the entity's action of killing them would involve corrupting the upper section of the surrounding area.

We are positive that The Glitcher was killing billions of people every day because he was looking for the descendants that unknowingly created him, with many known officials who were involved with the death and sealing of ████ and ████ were asked by The Glitcher if there were any living descendants of either ████ or ████. Witnesses who saw most officials who refuse or denied any claims of a direct descendant were brutally contorted beyond recognition with their bodies passing through themselves. Those who would provide information would be spared, with the cost of the rest of their lifespan will be spent in the dimension of the forgotten. (It is also to note that The Glitcher was able to kill beings that were beyond his strength and power, we are unsure on how he is capable to end beings who can erase him with just a thought.)
