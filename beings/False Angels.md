False Angels or FA is a species that are similar to [[xetrixes]], with the difference being they are created by another deity which is confirmed to be erased from time.

FA's are the much stronger counterpart of Xetrixes, using their abilities that are connected to the genetic code of reality, being masters of warping reality in a radius of 2³⁰ light years. They used to dominate the early periods of reality until the end of the [[heavenly war]], wiping almost all of them out of existence. The cause? We do not  really know, it is not possible for an entire species who master reality warping to vanish instantly in a single day, there were billions of them, they knew what they were doing.
