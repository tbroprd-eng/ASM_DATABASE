Funterna is a Primordial Deity that is the embodiment and an incarnation of power and energy, and  also the one to indirectly create another version of him by having 30% of his energy absorbed by [[HALLOWGEN]]. Funterna is also the founder and creator of the False angel space, which is a military like organization that is comprised out of [[False Angels]], with Funterna joining the [[VOT]] and merging [^1]FAS with it. 

### FALSE ANGEL SPACE
This section provides a summary of Funterna's history of being the Emperor of FAS. 

Funterna created the FAS even though he wasn't a False Angel, with one goal, to colonize, expand and reclaim spaces of Reality for pride and personal gain, he mostly did this for himself but still help the FAS to not make it obvious . 
While they did accomplish their goal before the [[heavenly war]] they still wanted power and control across existence, but they need one place for their base of operations, so Funterna created a dimension for all False Angels called "{DATA EXPUNGED BY THIRD PARTY}". With Funterna using this to duplicate and multiply clones of FA's.

During the events of the Heavenly war, Funterna would meet with VIDO, planning to join his Coalition to reclaim everything in reality, with it named "Void Of Tomorrow" or VOT for short. After 10 meetings, Funterna merged FAS with VOT, expanding its empire.

After the Heavenly War, the Dimension would collapse into itself, with Funterna being sealed with it
