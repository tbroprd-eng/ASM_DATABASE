Xetrix-SC are [[xetrixes]] that were modified by either [[False Angels]] that were taken to [[The Xetrix Armed Forces]] Camp or reality warpers that agreed to alter these xetrixes.

These Xetrixes would posses 7 abilities that heavily boost their previous characteristics and abilities such as their emotions, temper, strength, flight, swords, reaction time and sometimes even gives them a new ability.

While Xetrix-SC's have significantly better in all aspects, they do not have the ability to communicate with their voice or telepathy, with the their only way of speaking is morse code.
