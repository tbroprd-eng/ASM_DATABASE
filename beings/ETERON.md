Eteron is an Alternate universe anomaly of [[Xerxion]], who is a wanted fugitive across existence, with his bounty prize being ████████████████ in local currency. Eteron is also known for his unreal knowledge of almost any sort of information, with his body replaced with 40 self defense backup systems, including a modified version of [[IO]], as for what BRG, the director ASM said "This guy is just like me. Kill him. Kill anyone who helps him, kill anyone who is involved with him and make sure he is gone. He is a threat to our very existence".

### HISTORY
Eteron was unlike most Xerxions; he resented everything and believed he could control it all. He rejected joining any organization and committed acts of terror across his universe, purging all life he encountered. 
When Eteron exterminated all life in his universe, he got a visit from a deity, (we are unclear who it is due to Eteron saying it was "Confidential"), and it told Eteron about something, in which Eteron also said was confidential, but it was likely that the deity was telling him about alternate universes.
