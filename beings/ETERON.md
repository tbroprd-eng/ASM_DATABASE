Eteron is a Alternate universe anomaly of [[Xerxion]], who is a wanted fugitive across existence, with his bounty prize being ████████████████ in local currency. 

### Abilities
Eteron has multiple abilities, but one that strikes most is his ability to copy abilities that he maintain eye contacts with someone that is using the ability that is at least 2.15 meters in distance. He can enable this in advance in the number of 4.15 in any point of time(4.15 Seconds, minutes, hours, days, months, years, etc.) with the only drawbacks being once he sets a timer, he cant re-activate the ability until the ability activates, and how once he gains the abilities, he can only have the knowledge of the ability from the person who holds that ability specifically, he cant receive more knowledge from that ability from external parties.

### MOTIVE
Eterons motive to purge all Xerxion life is clear, Eteron hates Alternate versions of himself because they are "inferior", and that they think  that they are smart and have the free will of choice but they all stupidly reach to the same events that happen in all universes(eg., joining an alternate [[ASM]] , having similar powers that somehow have to do with matter manipulation powers, being friends to an Irisy, saving 1-20 more universes, etc.).

Eteron was not like most Xerxion's, he resented everything, and how he thinks everything is something he can control. He rejected joining any sort of organization and did terroristic acts on his universe, purging all life he sees. 

When Eteron exterminated all life in his universe, he got a visit from a deity, (we are unclear who it is due to Eteron saying it was "Confidential"), and it told Eteron about something, in which Eteron also said was confidential, but it was likely that the deity was telling him about alternate universe.
