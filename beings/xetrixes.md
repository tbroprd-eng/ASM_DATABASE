#HEXAGON 
### ABOUT
Xetrixes are known to be soldiers of the cosmos, protecting reality from threats during the early times of reality. They are also known for there strength being powerful enough to kill a God level threat with there bare hands.

### ORGINS
The xetrixes came from one being called [[Xatrix]], With Xatrix using 50% of his lifespan and physical body to make 56 Billion Xetrixes for the [[heavenly war]]. 

### APPEARANCE
Xetrixes look like humanoids that posses the appearance of a star  with vibrant colors that were never seen before. There average height is the planet of Jupiter to the size of 2 universes. They also carry a sword that can spilt anything in half like a knife that is hot cutting a block of butter.
