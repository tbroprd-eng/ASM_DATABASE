### about 

Handaranian, or called with their scientific name Manus Sentire are humanoid aliens who usually reside at Bx71. They are mostly known to be a very similar species to Humans, and also  having 58 countries on there home world and 122 more on 100 different planets.

The Handaranians are classified as a Type V civilization, being the 25th species to use multiple universes to harness energy.


### Appearance
Handaranians are bipedal humanoids who have malibu like skin color.
They also have an extra limb on their top right of their head, with a set of 3-7 unique yellow to orange stripes on their extra limb, Handaranians also have spikes on their back, shoulder to upper arm
