#HEXAGON  
### About 
Name: The Grabber
Designation: RC-008
Threat Level: Outerversal
Classification: Hypermanic God
Known Aliases:  
Vector of Origin:  Severed appendage of [[VIDO]]
Nature of Hazard: Conceptual
Core Objective: To strip Reality of Good.
Status: Eliminated

"Be alert and of sober mind. Your enemy the devil prowls around like a roaring lion looking for someone to *devour*"


A being made of pure evil and chaos energy,
it feeds of its victims and steals their abilities and energy using its unique ability: "Grab"
it has locked its eyes on a certain handaranian known as [[xerxion]] as it has the most powerful energy it has ever seen and wants to have it for itself to rule the multiverse. 

### Abilities
The Grabber has multiple abilities such as, absorbing almost anything it can see or think in a cosmic scale, meta-projection, warp reality and being able to be twice as strong as the opponent The Grabber is fighting
### History
The Grabber was one of the most powerful arms of [[VIDO]] with no sentience at all. When the most powerful arm of VIDO was cut during the [[heavenly war]], the arm gained sentience with 2 minds, both with the same goal to cause chaos everywhere, slowly they manifested a body in an unknown amount of time during the heavenly war, causing chaos around multiverses tormenting everything they see. Shortly after the events of VIDO being sealed, they were captured by high class [[xetrixes]] and Gods and was placed in a special area outside the fabric of reality, time and space to execute them. During the spell the xetrixes and Gods were chanting, one of them stuttered and messed it up, causing both minds of the arm to be split apart and manifested into there own bodies, one of them being [[the 6th God]] and the Grabber. They were quickly sent to the upper left and bottom right corner of reality for an indefinite amount of time.

During the events of Xerxion working with Barge. The Grabber escapes his confines to continue his chaos, and also figures out he can steal and absorb almost anything and gain powers from absorbing. After years of chaos, he finds someone who has the energy of [[scull]] which is xerxion and several others. He slowly kills most of them and absorbs there powers 
until Xerxion and 5 other people that are direct descendants to scull are left. The Grabber goes for Xerxion first due to how strong and connected he is to reality. Shortly after getting defeated by Xerxion, he gets imprisoned in a maximum dimension jail for how long and soon being put into execution again. When he was about to be executed, he kills everyone by absorbing there life. He then escapes the jail and now has a bounty an undisclosed amount of currency. During his run and soon rampage of everyone he sees, he finds one of the 5 people that are descendants of scull, which is a high ranking assassin in the multiverse. They fought for 3 and a half days and The grabber won by killing the assassin. He soon went to an undisclosed location to remerge with the 6th God and hibernate.

When the [[Second Heavenly War]] began, The Grabber wakes up in his true form in the exchange of killing and absorbing the 6th God, and feels the spike of void and follows the source. He then encounters someone he should have killed a long time ago, Xerxion. They did a rematch and fought for 13 hours. During the 12th hour and 58 minute, the Grabber was about to kill Xerxion who was severely weakened and injured, but then Xerxion gets dragged to a wormhole to another dimension, and a new challenger appears, which is Barge, in his form of Pure energy. After a quick fight, the Grabber dies in glory.
